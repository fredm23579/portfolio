About

I hold a Master's degree in Physics with research experience in plasma simulation and materials characterization. After years in theoretical physics, I completed a full-stack web development program at UCLA Extension and pivoted to software engineering — bringing with me the mathematical rigor, systems thinking, and first-principles reasoning that physics demands.

My current focus is building Agent-Omega, a governed recursive self-improvement platform that explores how AI systems can safely modify themselves under constitutional constraints. It sits at the intersection of everything I care about: formal verification, distributed systems, and the philosophy of autonomous reasoning.

I'm based in Riverside, California. I believe the best engineering comes from understanding the physics underneath.
